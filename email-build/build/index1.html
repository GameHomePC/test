Salesmsg Product Update
September 2020 New platform features and enhancements
HubSpot: Automatically Assign
Conversations Based on Contact Owner
Use Round Robin HubSpot Contact Owner Assignment feature to automatically assign the Shared Inbox conversation based on the HubSpot contact owner. Once applied, new conversations in the Shared Inbox will be assigned to the appropriate HubSpot contact owner automatically. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
Check It Out!
HubSpot: Show Activities on
Deal Record Timeline
Keeping track of Deal Activities has become even easier from your Deal Record Timeline. Now, if you are a HubSpot user, you can send a messages using Salesmsg Widget you will see a send record associated with an appropriate Deal in the Deal Record Timeline. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
Check It Out!
HubSpot: Show All Contacts
Associated to Deal
Keeping track of your Salesmsg Deal Contacts has become even more intuitive. Now, if you are a HubSpot user using Salesmsg Widget from the Deal View you will be able to see all Salesmsg contacts associated with the Deal. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
Check It Out!
HubSpot: Sidebar Widget Display
(Contacts + Deals) Update
Having more detailed message information on the HubSpot Sidebar Widget is a big time-saver. Now, if you are a HubSpot user you can see detailed message information such as Full Name, To & From, Message Status, Date and the Body of the Message itself on the Sidebar Widget on the Contacts and the Deals view. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
Check It Out!
Shared Inbox: Options on Filters
(Outbound, Closed)
Shared Inbox has always been one of the most popular features of the Salesmsg platform. Now, additional filtering functionality will give Shared Inbox users ability to use Outbound Filters to Assign or Close Shared Inbox messages. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
Check It Out!
