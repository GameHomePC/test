November 2020 New platform features and enhancements
Organizational Level Account Integration
Depth and breadth of the CRM integrations has always been the key differentiating feature of the Salesmsg Platform. As we continue to add various integration options tighter administrative controls become more and more essential. With this release the ability to connect and disconnect various integrated systems now belongs exclusively to Salemsg account admin. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!

HubSpot: Real Time Contact Search
If you are a HubSpot user we have great news for you. With this release we allow Salesmgs users to search for the Contact in the HubSpot CRM based on the Phone Number, Name or the Emails Address. If the contact is found it will be either linked with the corresponding contract in the Salesmgs or the new Contact will be created in the Salesmsg Platform. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!

Agency/Client Payment Method Options
Marketing and Sales agencies is the fastest growing segment of Salemsg customers. If you are an agency we offer a plethora of indispensable features to manage your own or associated client accounts. One of such features is an ability to manage Salemsg Platform payments for your associated clients directly or as a passthrough option. Whatever is your payment preference the choice is yours. AirCall: Shared Inboxes
Shared Inbox has always been one of the most popular features of the Salesmsg Platform. Until this release Shared Inbox functionality was only available to our Twilio customers. Now, our outstanding Shared Inbox functionality gives AirCall users ability to Triage, Auto-Assign and Filter Conversations in their Inbox. AirCall customers can also use Outbound Filters to Assign or Close Shared Inbox messages. Launch your app now to check it out for yourself!
